Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the nap sessions?

Our nap sessions typically range from 20 minutes (Power Nap) to 60 minutes (Relaxation and Luxury Nap). You can choose the duration that suits your needs.

Do you provide nap essentials like blankets and pillows?

Absolutely! We provide comfortable blankets and pillows to ensure your nap is as cozy as can be.

Can I book a nap in advance?

Yes, you can! We recommend booking your nap session in advance to secure your preferred time slot. Simply click the "Book Now" button on our website.

Are there any age restrictions for napping at Melad's?

Napping at Melad's is suitable for adults. We aim to create a serene and peaceful environment, and we kindly request that all guests be 18 years or older.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. We maintain clean and sanitized nap spaces, and our staff ensures that each area is properly disinfected between sessions. Masks are optional but encouraged for all guests.

Can I cancel or reschedule my nap session?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your nap session. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to make changes to your booking.

Are gift certificates available?

Yes, we offer gift certificates! Treat your friends and loved ones to the gift of relaxation with our custom gift certificates. Contact us for more details.
